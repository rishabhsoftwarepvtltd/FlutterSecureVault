# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We, as members, contributors, and leaders, pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity or expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We commit to:

* **Respect** each other's ideas, opinions, and contributions.
* **Communicate thoughtfully** and offer constructive feedback.
* **Foster inclusion** by being mindful of how our words and actions may affect others.
* **Avoid** any form of harassment, discrimination, or exclusion.

## Expected Behavior

* Use welcoming, inclusive, and professional language.
* Be patient and constructive during discussions and code reviews.
* Respect differing viewpoints, experiences, and skill levels.
* Gracefully accept constructive criticism.
* Support and encourage others in the community.

## Unacceptable Behavior

* Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination of any kind.
* Offensive, derogatory, or demeaning comments related to personal characteristics.
* Unwelcome sexual attention, advances, or inappropriate physical contact.
* Trolling, insults, or personal attacks.
* Publishing others' private information without explicit permission (e.g., doxxing).
* Deliberate disruption of community events, meetings, or communications.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers at **opensource@rishabhsoft.com**.

All complaints will be:

* Reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
* Handled with confidentiality to the maximum extent possible.
* Addressed with actions deemed appropriate by maintainers, which may include warnings, temporary bans, or permanent removal from the community.

Maintainers must enforce this Code of Conduct consistently across all project spaces, including issue trackers, pull requests, discussions, chats, and events.
